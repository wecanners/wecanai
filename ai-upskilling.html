Our method

Discover → Design → Deliver → Embed → Optimize

Five stages that turn scattered AI curiosity into a repeatable team capability.

01

Discover — AI readiness and workflow mapping

We assess your current workflows, pain points and risk constraints, and baseline AI literacy by role.

Deliverables: readiness snapshot · priority use cases · training plan outline

02

Design — a co-designed learning path by department

We build role-based tracks aligned to your objectives, language needs (English, Arabic, French) and internal processes.

Deliverables: program roadmap · lab scenarios · prompt templates · evaluation approach

03

Deliver — live training and hands-on labs

Your team learns by doing: live practice on real use cases, with feedback loops and quality checks.

Deliverables: reusable prompt packs · workflow checklists · "good output" standards

04

Embed — make AI repeatable

We turn learning into a system: templates, SOPs, review steps and adoption guardrails.

Deliverables: workflow playbooks · governance-lite guidelines · usage do's and don'ts

05

Optimize — keep workflows current (optional)

As tools change, your workflows stay current. We support iteration, refresh sessions and continuous improvement.

What your team learns

From safe foundations to advanced execution

AI literacy

What AI can and cannot do and where it fails; how to verify outputs and reduce hallucinations; responsible use around confidentiality and approvals; and which model to choose for which job.

Prompt engineering

Prompt structure for consistent results; few-shot prompting for repeatable formats; retrieval-assisted prompting for knowledge-heavy work; and multimodal workflows across text, image and document tasks.

Implementation skills

The part most training skips: turning prompts into reusable templates, building AI-ready SOPs and review loops, document automation, and measuring adoption and quality improvements.
