Our programs

Four ways to put AI to work in your organization.

Every program is customized, lab-based and trilingual. Each one can be delivered on-site, virtually or blended, and is scoped to your team's size, sector and goals.

Discuss the right fit → See our method
01

AI Literacy Essentials

A short introductory workshop for teams new to AI.

For teams who feel “AI is everywhere but we don't know what to do with it” and need a safe, confident foundation fast.

  • What AI genuinely does, and where it fails
  • Safe use: confidentiality, verification, judgment
  • First practical prompts for everyday tasks
  • A shared baseline the whole team starts from

Delivered on-site, virtually or blended · scoped to your team

02

Core Upskilling Program

Our flagship literacy, prompt engineering and embedding journey.

For teams that want a repeatable AI capability, not one-off luck, built around the work they actually do.

  • AI literacy and critical evaluation of outputs
  • Advanced, structured prompt engineering
  • Embedding: templates, SOPs, guardrails, QA checks
  • Hands-on labs on your real workflows

Delivered on-site, virtually or blended · scoped to your team

03

Leadership AI Briefing

A focused session for executives and decision-makers.

For leaders who need to set AI direction, policy and expectations with confidence, without a full technical program.

  • Where AI creates value, and where it creates risk
  • What good governance and an operating standard look like
  • How to evaluate tools, vendors and proposals
  • A clear view of what to ask of your teams

Delivered on-site, virtually or blended · scoped to your team

04

Train-the-Trainer

For larger organizations building internal capacity.

For organizations that want AI champions of their own, able to sustain and scale adoption after the engagement ends.

  • Deeper mastery of literacy and prompt engineering
  • How to teach, facilitate and run AI labs internally
  • Reusable materials and a delivery playbook
  • A self-sufficient internal AI capability

Delivered on-site, virtually or blended · scoped to your team
