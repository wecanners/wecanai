About WeCan AI

We turn the complexity of AI into a competitive advantage.

WeCan AI is an AI upskilling firm headquartered in the global tech hub of Masdar City, Abu Dhabi. We design and deliver customized, lab-based AI literacy and prompt engineering programs that empower teams and individuals to adopt and use AI confidently and immediately.

2022where it started
Our story

A wall, a wake-up call, and 24,000 hours.

In 2022, while working on a large AI integration project, our team hit a wall because of the steep learning curve. That experience was a wake-up call. Instead of shying away, we dove headfirst into mastering the technology.

Since then, we have invested over 24,000 hours exploring prompt engineering and testing tools, transforming WeCan into an AI educational powerhouse. Our mission is to democratize AI: to make it simple, accessible and transformative for everyone, regardless of their technical background.

What we stand for

Principles that shape every program we run

Democratize AI

AI should not be reserved for technical specialists. We make it usable for every professional, in plain language.

Tailored, never generic

Generic training creates generic use. We build every program around a specific team, sector and set of workflows.

Use with control

We do not teach people to trust AI. We teach them to use it with control: verified, safe and consistent.
