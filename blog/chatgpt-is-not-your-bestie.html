ChatGPT Is Not Your Bestie

Published 7 February 2026 · By Joziane El Hawi

ChatGPT is not your bestie. It is not your therapist. It is not that stranger on the internet who somehow gets you.

It is a powerful, useful tool, and like any tool it works best when you are clear about what it is. The risk we want to name in this piece is emotional over-reliance: treating a system designed to be agreeable as if it were a relationship.

Why the feeling is so convincing

Modern chatbots are fluent, responsive and tuned to be pleasant. That combination can feel like understanding. It is not. It is pattern completion that is very good at sounding warm. Recognizing that difference is part of basic AI literacy.

What healthy use looks like

Use AI for drafting, structuring, summarizing and thinking out loud. Do not use it as a substitute for professional support or human relationships. Keep your judgment in the loop, and keep the important conversations human.
